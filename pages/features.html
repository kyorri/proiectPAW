Features of our humble platform

Friends' Blogs

Users can connect with other users and receive suggestions for articles from their friends' blogs.

Trending Blogs

Popular articles are highlighted on the Trending Blogs page, similar to viral content on social media platforms.

Create Articles

Users can create articles, specify collaborating users, and attach multiple photo attachments on the Create page.

Profile

The Profile page displays various parameters of a user, including articles created by the user, attached images, article modifications, and user profile description.

FAQs

The FAQs page provides answers to frequently asked questions in an accordion-style menu.

Notifications

Users receive notifications for activities such as likes on their articles, adding friends, and platform announcements like maintenance updates.
